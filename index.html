About the Club

Notton FC is more than just a football team. We are a close-knit, family-orientated club that brings people together through the love of the game and community spirit.

Who We Are

We are a welcoming football club where players, families, and supporters all play a part in the journey.

What We Stand For

Commitment, sportsmanship, inclusion, and creating a positive environment both on and off the pitch.

Why Join

Whether you play, volunteer, sponsor, or support from the sidelines, you are part of The Red Lions family.
